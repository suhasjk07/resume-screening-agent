Job Title: SOC L1 Analyst (Security Operations Center)
Location: Bengaluru, India (Hybrid)
Experience: 0-2 years

About the Role
We are hiring a Tier-1 Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst to monitor, triage, and
respond to security alerts across our enterprise environment. This is a 24x7 shift-based
role suited to someone who wants hands-on exposure to real-world threat detection and
incident response.

Required Skills
- SIEM tools: Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, or QRadar
- Strong understanding of networking fundamentals (TCP/IP, DNS, VPN, firewalls)
- Familiarity with the MITRE ATT&CK framework
- Log analysis and alert triage (Windows Event Logs, Linux syslog)
- Basic scripting for automation (Python or PowerShell)
- Understanding of common attack vectors: phishing, malware, brute force, lateral movement
- Knowledge of endpoint detection tools (EDR) such as CrowdStrike or Microsoft Defender

Preferred Qualifications
- CompTIA Security+, CCNA, or equivalent certification (in progress is acceptable)
- Exposure to firewall platforms such as FortiGate or Palo Alto
- Prior internship or lab experience with incident response playbooks
- Familiarity with KQL (Kusto Query Language) for Sentinel-based hunting
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, Cybersecurity, or a related field

Responsibilities
- Monitor SIEM dashboards and triage incoming security alerts
- Perform initial investigation and escalate confirmed incidents to L2/L3
- Document findings and maintain accurate incident tickets
- Follow standard operating procedures (SOPs) and escalation playbooks
- Assist with report generation for weekly security posture reviews
